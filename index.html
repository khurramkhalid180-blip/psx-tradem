▸ 4 strategies — how they work and why they make money
Only Grade A (4+ factors) and Grade B (3+ factors) setups shown. Weak signals hidden.
Position sizing for 1% account risk is shown on every card.

ORDER BLOCK — Enter at institutional origin zones. Institutions leave unfilled orders where they started their moves. Stop just beyond the block. Highest win rate when in confirmed trend.

STOP HUNT — Enter after a liquidity sweep reversal. Market makers push price beyond highs/lows to trigger stops, then reverse. You enter AFTER the manipulation. Tight stop, high R:R.

FVG — Fair Value Gap bounce IN THE TREND DIRECTION ONLY. Counter-trend FVGs are filtered out. Divergence at FVG = very high probability.

ORB — Opening Range Breakout requires 1.8× volume minimum. Low-volume ORBs are penalised. VWAP above/below the OR adds major confluence.

⚡ Why Live Prices Need Vercel
PSX blocks direct browser requests (CORS). The solution is a Vercel serverless function that fetches PSX from the server side and passes data to your browser — no CORS issue, no bookmarklet, fully automatic.

Vercel is 100% free for this usage. Your app gets a permanent URL like https://psx-tradem.vercel.app accessible on phone, PC, anywhere.
🚀 Deploy in 5 Minutes — Step by Step
STEP 1 — Download Project Files
Download the psx-tradem.zip file below. It contains:
📁 api/prices.js  ← serverless proxy
📁 public/index.html  ← this dashboard
📄 vercel.json  ← config
📄 package.json  ← metadata
STEP 2 — Create Free GitHub Account
Go to github.com/signup (free)
Create account → verify email → done
STEP 3 — Create Repository & Upload Files
1. Go to github.com/new
2. Name it: psx-tradem
3. Set to Public → click Create repository
4. Click "uploading an existing file"
5. Drag ALL files from the zip → click Commit changes
STEP 4 — Deploy on Vercel
1. Go to vercel.com/signup (free)
2. Sign up with your GitHub account (1 click)
3. Click "Add New Project"
4. Select your psx-tradem repository
5. Click Deploy — wait ~60 seconds
6. ✅ Vercel gives you a URL: https://psx-tradem-xxxx.vercel.app
STEP 5 — Open Your URL on Phone & PC
Open your Vercel URL on any device.
📱 iPhone/Android: Share → "Add to Home Screen" (works like an app)
💻 PC: Bookmark it
🔄 Auto-updates: Every time you push to GitHub, Vercel redeploys in 30 seconds
📊 Portfolio: Saved in browser localStorage — persists across sessions on same device
